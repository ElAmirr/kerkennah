Location

The islands are warm and dry, with strong prevailing winds. This is mainly due to its positioning in the Gulf of Gabes, with strong sea winds (Gharbi), carried over the mainland, making them hot and dry. What little water vapour there is, is lost over cooler Tunisia first. This causes the general ecology of the island to mainly consist of tall xerophytic (and often halophytic) flora, such as palms and saltbushes. The land is arid and there is little agriculture, though the islanders own chickens and goats for their own personal consumption. Fishing, especially for octopus, is a key industry of Kerkennah, whence it is exported to mainland Tunisia and other nearby countries. Tourism is limited in Kerkennah and it lacks grandiose sandy beaches. Many mainland Tunisians spend their holidays in Kerkennah, and many more affluent Tunisians often build private second homes on the island. Tourists also come from European countries. Temperatures on the island are high, with a minimum of 4 °C (39 °F) and often reaching 40 °C (104 °F).

Accomodations

Cercina Hotel

Such a quiete place, charming landscapes, very nice rooms in harmony with the style presented.. the views are incredible there, you cannot miss the sunset there, the most beautiful scene you may ever see .. ❤ The service is good, people are very welcoming and smiling all day long.. The restaurant is very well known of its kind, it offers sea food varieties I fell in love with, what a paradise!!

Dar Kerkennah Hotel

Amazingly charming little hotel!! The rooms were spacious, with all necessities, the personnel went above and beyond - they were super nice, helpful and at the same time very professional. The dinners were also very tasty, from fresh local ingredients and at very reasonable prices. All in all, the hotel was a pleasant surprise and I wouldn’t hesitate to visit again!

Le Grand Hotel

A big and comfortable hotel right on the sea with a swimming pool. It also has a cultural program. It is not crowded with tourists from outside Tunisia, meaning a place on the beach, in the sea or in the swimming pool will always be found. Good atmosphere and food. Nice atmosphere. Who does not have high demands and wants to relax is really recommend.

Ennakhla Hotel

An exceptional hotel with a family atmosphere an excellent non-alcoholic hotel service. Suitable for families. Friendly personal. The pool is small but there is access to the beach which is 50m.
